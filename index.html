Skin care

We present the following tips to keep your skin healthy and smooth looking : Drink a lot of water you need to drink two to three liters a day.
Water helps the body to function as much as possible (two thirds of our body is water) and makes your skin smoother and fresher.
Cleaning skin Use a dry brush to gently scrub your skin to remove dead cells that can damage your skin.
Sun protection Skin cancer poses the greatest risk from prolonged sun exposure.
Moisturizing your skin daily will help keep it smooth by increasing moisture levels.
Skin has different needs depending on age and skin type, so if you are not sure which is best for you, it is recommended that you consult with a pharmacist.

hair care

Use a hair conditioner after each use of the shampoo, taking into account the selection of the type that suits the type of hair, its length, and the problems it may suffer from.
Moisturizing the hair with a hair conditioner after washing it, and combing it with a wide-toothed comb before drying it.
This is to soften it and get rid of tangles inside it.
Use a piece of cotton clothing to dry your hair instead of using a regular towel.
Bathing with warm water, where taking a shower using warm water is one of the methods of hair care, as it is possible to massage the scalp
with warm water while washing the hair using shampoo.
